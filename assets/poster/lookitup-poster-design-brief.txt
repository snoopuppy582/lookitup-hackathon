LookItUp Poster Design Brief

Purpose
- One poster for the hackathon presentation requirement.
- Must include the problem, catchline, group name, and product idea.

Group Name
- Don't Look Up

Product Name
- LookItUp

Catchline
- LookItUp: Turn doubt into evidence.

Core Claim
- LookItUp creates a source boundary before publication.

Problem
- Claims move faster than evidence.
- Main case: AI-generated Kamchatka snow visuals crossed platforms and newsrooms.

Design Elements Extracted From The Reference Image
- Canvas: 16:9 landscape poster.
- Background: near-black charcoal.
- Accent color: soft mint green.
- Text color: white, with mint highlights for key words.
- Typography: heavy rounded sans-serif; large, friendly, high-contrast.
- Logo placement: top-left.
- Layout: left side carries the main problem sentence; right side carries evidence blocks.
- Visual rhythm: oversized numbers or short blocks, not paragraphs.
- Footer: team name on bottom-left, source or project note centered, small page mark bottom-right.
- Strong whitespace.
- No decorative illustration.
- No dense boxes.

Recommended Poster Layout
- Top-left: LookItUp logo.
- Top-right: Don't Look Up · Korea & France.
- Left column: "The problem?" and one large sentence.
- Right side: three large blocks showing the product flow:
  1. Claim
  2. Trusted Sources
  3. Evidence Cards
- Bottom center: catchline.
- Bottom-left: group name.
- Bottom-right: demo URL or page number.

Poster Copy
- The problem?
- Claims move faster than evidence.
- LookItUp creates a source boundary.
- Claim -> Trusted Sources -> Evidence Cards
- LookItUp: Turn doubt into evidence.
- Don't Look Up · Korea & France
