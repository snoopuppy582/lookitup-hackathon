# LookItUp

## Core Message

**LookItUp creates a source boundary before publication.**

## Tagline

**LookItUp: Turn doubt into evidence.**

## Team Name

**Don't Look Up**

## One-Liner

LookItUp stops unverified claims before they become published news.

## What We Show

A journalist selects a Trusted Source Pack, searches one doubtful claim, and receives Evidence Cards before publishing.

The demo is one loop:

> Trusted Source Pack -> Claim Search -> Evidence Cards -> Publish Decision

## Why It Matters

A fake AI snow visual from Russia's Kamchatka Peninsula crossed platforms and reached Korean media before the evidence trail was visible.

The newsroom failure is simple:

> A claim reached publication before evidence caught up.

LookItUp gives the journalist a source boundary before that claim goes live.
